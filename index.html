Coach Ahman Elliott-Hunter better known as Coach O is a nationally recognized amateur boxing coach with over 15 yrs of experience. He's currently the Junior Olympic Co-Chairman for USABOXING NJ and was honored by the NJ Boxing Hall of Fame named 2018 Coach of the Year. He has a love for the sweet science of boxing and is dedicated to helping men, women, and children not only become champions inside the ring, but become champions in life. Coach O's two oldest children, KahShad, top-ranked 2020 USA Boxing Olympic Trialist and Kaheil, USA Boxing Youth Women's Team Member both train at Strap City Boxing Club. He has been the National Coach for Team NJ (Junior, Youth, & Elite Level Boxers) since 2010.
